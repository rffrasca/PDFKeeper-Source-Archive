Accessing the Direct Upload Folder and sub-folders

  • To access the Direct Upload Folder and all configured sub-folders for performing file level operations (rename, delete, etc.), click the Tools menu, and then click Direct Upload Folder; or you can use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Shift+D.
  • To configure Direct Upload sub-folders (add, edit, and delete), click the Tools menu, and then click Direct Upload Configuration; or you can use the keyboard shortcut Ctrl+D.
